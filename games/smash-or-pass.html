Smash or Pass

How to Play: Smash or Pass

Smash or Pass is a classic party game that challenges a participant to decide whether they would 'Smash' or 'Pass' a particular person.

The participant will be prompted with a person and they must choose if they'd 'Smash' or 'Pass'

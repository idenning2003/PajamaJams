How to Play: Pictionary

Pictionary is an entertaining drawing and guessing game perfect for groups of friends or family.

Start by dividing players into teams. Each team selects a drawer for the round.

On each turn, the designated drawer clicks the "New Prompt" button and attempts to draw whatever is promted in the box while their team tries to guess what it is.

The drawer cannot use words, letters, or gestures in their drawings.

If the team correctly guesses the word within a time limit, they earn points. If not, the opposing team gets a chance to steal.

The game continues with players taking turns drawing until a team reaches the designated point goal.

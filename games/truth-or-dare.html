How to Play: Truth or Dare

Truth or Dare is a classic party game that invites participants to engage in a series of revealing questions and daring challenges.

To play, gather a group of friends and sit together in a circle. Decide who will go first, either by volunteering or by random selection. The first player then chooses whether they want to answer a truth question or perform a dare.

If they choose truth, they must truthfully answer the question posed by the prompt.

If they choose dare, they must complete a challenge assigned by the prompt.

After completing their turn, the next player takes their turn, and the game continues in this manner. The game often leads to laughter, surprises, and deeper connections among participants as they share personal insights and embark on adventurous challenges together.
